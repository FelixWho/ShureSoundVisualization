Tim is a proof of concept of audio visualization and audio quality testing

Intentions

To test a room for its audio-recording capabilities and to visualize audio.

Stuff We Did

Audio Visualization

We use Three.js to display audio frequencies with 3D cubes and change the background color when lower frequencies register.

Audio Quality Measurement

Using Python and novel algorithms, we compare discrete fourier transform outputs of idealized and realized sound clips.

GCP

We use GCP to create an Apache server with flask backend and our own API endpoints in order to stream audio.

Lots of Math

For example, we balance our audio input by frequency variance and median to ensure our visualization looks consistent.
